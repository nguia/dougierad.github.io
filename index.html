ItemFindr

  • Using the ItemFindr app, users can search for products in a supermarket and find their exact location.
  • Store maps can be viewed to find where general categories of items can be found.
  • Users can add items to shopping lists, and the app will provide routes for getting all listed items.
  • If an item is out of stock, users can flag the item through the app, which will let the store's grocery staff, as well as other users, know.
